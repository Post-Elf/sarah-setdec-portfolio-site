About

I am a Set Decorator and Stylist creating considered, character-led spaces for feature film, TV, commercials, stills and events. This job is magic.

After studying Theatre Design at Wimbledon College of Art, I began my career casting Supporting Artists for many blockbuster feature films, before finding my way into the art department.

BA (1st) Performance Design, Wimbledon College of Art, 2011 · IMDb for Film & TV credits

I have worked on projects for Amazon, HBO, Apple, Netflix, Partizan, BBC, Columbia Pictures, ITN, Big Talk, Tantrum, Disney, ThomasThomas, Battersea Arts Centre, Warner Bros, Working Title, ITV, Secret Cinema, Esquire, Architecture Foundation, Beast, RSA, Smith & Jones, LadBible, Biscuit, Agile, Another, Bray Leino, StudioCanal and Sony Columbia, among others.
